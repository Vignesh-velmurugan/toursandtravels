Chennai

Chennai, formerly known as Madras, is the capital city of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is located on the southeastern coast of India, overlooking the Bay of Bengal. Known for its vibrant culture, Chennai is a blend of tradition and modernity. It boasts of historical landmarks like the Fort St. George, Marina Beach - one of the longest urban beaches in the world, and the iconic Kapaleeshwarar Temple. Chennai is also a major hub for the Tamil film industry, often referred to as "Kollywood," and is renowned for its Carnatic music and Bharatanatyam dance. The city's cuisine is diverse, with a strong emphasis on South Indian flavors, including delicious dosas, idlis, and spicy curries. With its bustling streets, rich heritage, and warm hospitality, Chennai offers visitors a unique and memorable experience.
